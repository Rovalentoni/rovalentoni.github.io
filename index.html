About Me

My name is Rodrigo, I'm a U.S. Permanent Resident with family roots in Colorado.
From a young age, my logical reasoning and problem-solving abilities have naturally aligned me with the field of software development. While completing my academic pursuits in Computer Engineering, I've gained over a year of hands-on experience in the software industry.

My skill set is not limited to JavaScript and PHP; it also includes server management with Docker and Linux, as well as experience in implementing RESTful APIs and SOA in ERP systems with integrated BPM functionalities. Through my projects, I've applied rigorous testing and systematic approaches to ensure robustness and scalability, particularly in the optimization of ERP systems and leveraging BPM workflows.

My career aim is to become a Full Stack Developer, with a focus on Backend and AI technologies.
